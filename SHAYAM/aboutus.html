About Us

Our mission is to make our community a better place by serving as an advocate for companion humans. Our vision for the future is that no person is left unattended during times of calamity or emergency. We aim to create a platform where anyone in need can get the help they need timely.

History

A community developed by group of humanitarian to provide a safer environment to all people.Our only purpose was to ensure that no one is left unattended in situations of crisis .

We aim to collect donations that are dispatched to people in need at time of distress. People may join us voluntarily to provide aid to those in need of immediate help. We wish to grow not just as an organisation but as a community that operates country wide. It is correctly said that small initiatives turn into a bigger better idea which helps to mold the society thereby making it better place to live.
