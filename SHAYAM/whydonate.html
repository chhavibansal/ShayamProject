Why are we asking for donation?

We believe in holding the trust of our donars,thereby we put in all the money donated to us for the wellfare of humans who have been affected by any kind of accident, and who do not have sufficient resources to work their way through. Your continued support is a source of our continous help provided to physically wounded, terror struck, family seperated people. We put in the money to provide such people with shelter, food, aminities of their needs and basic medical facilities until they reunite with their families.Your donation stays with the organisation until we find someone needy.



Thank You for your CONTINUED Support

Where Do We Use Your Funds?

During time of calamity a lot of people lose their lifelong earning, and it takes a lot of time to earn it back, so as humanitarian we can extend our hand to help people in need. So here we are with a solution which will surely not heart your pocket and also help those in deadly need of money. As brothers and sisters we get up and take this as out responsibility to help them and get their life back to normal. So a small contribution from your side will be a huge help to the society.

We may not be responsible for the plight of these stray animals but we can definetly treat them and help them live a better life. The more we spend on things of charity the more we flourish. It is a humble request from our side that, even a small amount can be of great help.
