OUR INITIATIVE

Emergencies can occur anytime, anywhere. From a damages vehicle to a natural disaster, such emergency situations can affect you and your loved ones. Welcome to our website, a community of people trying to be there for one another when needed. Come and join us and help us report emergencies and handle them. Welcome.

Welcome to सहाय्यम्

So what is the AIM of SAHAYAM directory? Our mission is to make our community a better place for people of all sections. We are willing working to build a society wherein no person is left unattended during time of calamity and a compassionate society is formed. People have joined us from various domains providing help from their end, we are counting on you to join us and make this mission a vision.

So... who are we?

We are a charitable firm who run solely by volunteers.During time of calamity we want to reach out to people who need us, but due to our family needs we end up being of no use to them. So here is a portal where such like minded people join hands and contribute money that is reached out to people of need . You can donate a vast variety of items raning from cloth to food to even monetary help, or more better educate a child who is abandoned by his family.

We also occasionally have meetups to spread awarness about our initiative and to reach out to larger masses who are looking for a medium like ours. If, for example, we know we're coming to Delhi, we'll let everyone know and schedule a meet up day to tour the city and take photos. If you'd like us to visit your city, let us know!
