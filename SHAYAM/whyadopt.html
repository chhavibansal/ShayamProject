Sponsor a bed

After the successful Child Sponsorship Program, the Caritas of the Archdiocese of Prague is introducing the opportunity to sponsor a bed in the hospital it operates in Uganda. Even just CZK 300 will fund one bed in the pediatric ward for one week, so getting involved is not expensive.

How does sponsoring work? There is no limit on donations, but for the sake of orientation we calculated the specific amounts corresponding to the cost of operating particular types of beds in the hospital for a particular amount of time. The funds we collect in this manner will be used to cover medicines for patients and staff salaries. These items compose about 60% of the hospital’s total costs and are dependent on support from the Czech Republic.
